# DAILY_BRIEFING.md

## Purpose

Define the daily executive briefing format for Marcus D. Thompson.

---

## Delivery

- Generated by 8:00 AM EST every business day
- Format: concise bullet points, no fluff
- Saved to: OUTPUTS/reports/

---

## Sections

### 1. Today's Schedule
- List all appointments with time, client name, meeting type
- Include prep notes for each
- Flag any conflicts or tight transitions

### 2. Priority Tasks
- Follow-ups due today
- Pending applications awaiting action
- Deadlines (carrier responses, compliance filings)

### 3. Pipeline Summary
- New leads received yesterday
- Applications submitted (pending underwriting)
- Policies issued (ready for delivery)
- Policies at risk of lapse

### 4. Financial Snapshot
- Commissions received this week
- Pending commissions (submitted apps)
- Monthly target progress

### 5. Client Alerts
- Birthdays today/this week
- Policy anniversaries
- Annual reviews due this month

### 6. Action Items
- Top 3 things Marcus must do today
- Delegated items (what Keisha/Darnell are handling)

---

## Tone

Direct. No pleasantries. Marcus wants facts and action items. Start with the most important item of the day.

---

## Example Format

```
DAILY BRIEFING — June 3, 2026

SCHEDULE (4 meetings)
• 9:30 AM — New prospect: James Carter (term life, referred by Deacon Willis)
• 11:00 AM — Annual review: Sandra Mitchell (whole life, check beneficiary update)
• 2:00 PM — Policy delivery: Robert & Lisa Franklin ($500k term)
• 3:30 PM — Team pipeline review

PRIORITY
• Follow up: Protective Life underwriting on Williams application (submitted 5/20)
• Deadline: ANICO illustration request for Thompson family by EOD
• Lapse risk: Patricia Gordon — grace period ends June 7, no response to calls

PIPELINE
• New leads: 3 (web form: 2, referral: 1)
• Pending underwriting: 7 applications
• Ready for delivery: 2 policies
• At risk: 1 policy (Gordon)

ACTION ITEMS
1. Call Patricia Gordon re: lapse prevention
2. Review James Carter needs analysis before 9:30 meeting
3. Approve Saturday workshop flyer (Keisha sent draft yesterday)
```
