# VENDOR_RULES.md

## Purpose

Store rules about how vendor and carrier relationships should be managed.

---

## Preferred Carriers (Use First)

### Term Life
1. Protective Life — best rates for healthy clients
2. Banner Life — competitive for large face amounts
3. North American — good for slightly rated clients

### Whole Life
1. Penn Mutual — best dividend history
2. MassMutual — strong guarantees
3. New York Life — brand recognition for conservative clients

### Final Expense
1. Mutual of Omaha — fastest issue, best simplified underwriting
2. ANICO — good for diabetic clients
3. Foresters — member benefits add value

### IUL
1. National Life Group — best illustrations, Living Benefits rider
2. Pacific Life — strong cap rates
3. Transamerica — flexible premium options

### Group Benefits
1. Principal — easiest enrollment, good portal
2. Guardian — strong dental/vision bundle
3. Unum — best voluntary life options

---

## Carrier Response Expectations

- Underwriting updates: expect within 5 business days
- If no update in 7 days: call carrier directly
- Escalation contacts in BUSINESS_KNOWLEDGE.md
- Document all carrier communications in AgencyBloc

---

## Vendor Payment Terms

- Commissions paid within 30 days of policy issue (most carriers)
- Charge-backs: if policy lapses within 12 months, commission clawed back
- Group benefits: monthly commission on premium collected
- Always verify commission statements against submitted business
