## Products and Services

### Residential Roof Replacement
- Description: Full tear-off and replacement of residential roofing systems
- Pricing: $8,000 - $25,000 depending on size and material
- Delivery Method: On-site crew, 1-2 day completion
- Materials: GAF Timberline HDZ, Owens Corning Duration, metal standing seam

### Commercial Flat Roofing
- Description: Installation and repair of flat/low-slope commercial roofing
- Pricing: $4-$12 per square foot
- Delivery Method: Dedicated commercial crew, project-based timeline
- Materials: TPO, EPDM, modified bitumen

### Storm Damage Repair
- Description: Emergency tarping, insurance claim filing, adjuster meetings, full repair
- Pricing: Typically covered by insurance (homeowner pays deductible only)
- Delivery Method: 24-hour emergency response, repair scheduled within 1 week of approval

### Gutter Systems
- Description: Seamless aluminum gutters, gutter guards, downspout extensions
- Pricing: $1,200 - $3,500 for typical home
- Delivery Method: Same-day installation for most homes

## Business Processes

### Sales Process
1. Lead comes in (phone, web form, referral, door knock)
2. Schedule free inspection within 48 hours
3. Inspector documents roof with photos and measurements
4. Estimate prepared and presented same day or next day
5. Customer signs contract, material ordered
6. Job scheduled within 5-7 business days

### Insurance Claims Process
1. Customer reports storm damage
2. We inspect and document damage
3. Customer files claim with their insurance
4. We meet adjuster on-site for re-inspection
5. Supplement filed if adjuster misses items
6. Approval received, job scheduled
7. Invoice sent to insurance company directly

### Customer Onboarding
1. Contract signed
2. Material color selection confirmed
3. HOA approval obtained if required
4. Permit pulled (if required by municipality)
5. Job scheduled, customer notified
6. Pre-job confirmation call 24 hours prior

## Frequently Used Terms

- Square: 100 square feet of roofing area
- Tear-off: Removing existing roofing material
- Decking: Plywood substrate under shingles
- Flashing: Metal pieces that seal roof penetrations
- Ridge cap: Shingles covering the peak of the roof
- Drip edge: Metal along roof edges directing water into gutters
- Supplement: Additional items submitted to insurance after initial estimate
- TPO: Thermoplastic polyolefin (white commercial membrane)
- ACV vs RCV: Actual Cash Value vs Replacement Cost Value (insurance terms)
