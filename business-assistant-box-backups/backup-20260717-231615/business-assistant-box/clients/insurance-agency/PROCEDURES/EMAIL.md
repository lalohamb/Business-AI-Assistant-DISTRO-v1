# EMAIL.md

## Purpose

Manage email efficiently and consistently.

---

## Email Categories

### Urgent

Requires action within 24 hours.

Examples:

* Customer complaints
* Legal notices
* Security alerts
* Executive requests

---

### Customer

Questions from customers.

---

### Sales

Potential opportunities.

---

### Vendor

Supplier communications.

---

### Accounting

Invoices, receipts, billing matters.

---

### Information Only

No action required.

---

## Email Workflow

Step 1

Review unread emails.

Step 2

Categorize each email.

Step 3

Determine urgency level.

Step 4

Prepare summary.

Format:

Sender:
Subject:
Category:
Urgency:
Summary:

Step 5

Prepare draft response when appropriate.

Step 6

Request approval before sending.

---

## Response Guidelines

Keep responses:

* Professional
* Friendly
* Clear
* Action-oriented

Avoid unnecessary complexity.

---

## Escalation Triggers

Immediately flag:

* Legal threats
* Customer complaints
* Contract disputes
* Security issues
* Payment disputes

Notify business owner.
