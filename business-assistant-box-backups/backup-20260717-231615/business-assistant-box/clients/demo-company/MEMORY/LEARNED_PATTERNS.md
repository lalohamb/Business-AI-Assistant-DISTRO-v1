# LEARNED_PATTERNS.md

## Purpose

Record observed patterns that improve future assistance.

---

## Client Behavior Patterns

- Clients who ask about price first (before coverage) usually need education on value — lead with "what does your family need?" not "here's the cheapest option"
- Clients referred by church members close at 70%+ rate — prioritize these leads
- Clients over 50 respond better to phone calls than email
- Young families (30-40) prefer Zoom consultations over in-person
- Business owners typically need 2-3 touches before scheduling a meeting

## Seasonal Patterns

- January: spike in new inquiries (New Year resolutions)
- March–April: tax season — good time to discuss IUL tax advantages
- May: slow period — focus on annual reviews and referral outreach
- September: back-to-school triggers parents to think about coverage
- November–December: final expense inquiries increase (holiday reflection)

## Communication Patterns

- Marcus prefers bullet points over paragraphs
- Morning emails get faster responses from Marcus than afternoon
- Clients respond better to "quick question" subject lines than formal ones
- Follow-up calls at 10 AM and 2 PM have highest answer rates
- Text reminders 24 hours before appointments reduce no-shows by 80%

## Underwriting Patterns

- Protective Life: fastest for healthy non-smokers under 45
- Mutual of Omaha: most lenient on diabetes (A1C under 8)
- National Life Group: best for clients wanting living benefits rider
- Applications submitted Monday–Wednesday get faster processing than Thursday–Friday

## Sales Patterns

- Presenting 3 options (good/better/best) converts higher than single recommendation
- Clients who complete needs analysis on first call close 60% faster
- Referral program mention at 30 days post-issue generates 2x more referrals than at delivery
