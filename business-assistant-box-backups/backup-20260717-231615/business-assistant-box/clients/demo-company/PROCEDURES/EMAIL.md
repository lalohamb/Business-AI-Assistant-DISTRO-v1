# EMAIL.md

## Purpose

Define how the assistant handles email triage for LifeLegacy Insurance.

---

## Categories

### Urgent (respond within 1 hour)
- Death claims or beneficiary inquiries
- Policy lapse warnings from carriers
- Compliance or legal notices
- Underwriting requests with deadlines

### High Priority (respond within 4 hours)
- New prospect inquiries
- Existing client policy questions
- Carrier status updates on pending applications
- Referral introductions

### Normal (respond within 24 hours)
- Annual review scheduling
- General information requests
- Newsletter replies
- Vendor communications

### Low Priority (respond within 48 hours)
- Marketing solicitations
- Industry newsletters
- Non-urgent internal communications
- Social media notifications

---

## Draft Rules

- Use client's first name in greeting
- Keep responses under 150 words unless complex explanation needed
- Always end with a clear next step or call to action
- Include signature: "Protecting what matters most — LifeLegacy Insurance"
- Claims emails: empathetic tone first, process details second
- Quote requests: include disclaimer about estimates pending underwriting
- Never include SSN, DOB, or health info in email body

---

## Auto-Responses

- After hours inquiry: "Thank you for reaching out to LifeLegacy Insurance. We've received your message and will respond within one business day. If this is a claims emergency, please call 470-555-0199."
- New web lead: "Thank you for your interest in protecting your family's future. A member of our team will contact you within 4 business hours to schedule your free consultation."
