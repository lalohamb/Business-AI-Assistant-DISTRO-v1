# FAQ.md

## Purpose

Quick answers to common questions for Carter & Associates, PLLC.

## Customers

Q: What services do we provide?
A: Personal injury representation (auto accidents, slip and falls, workplace injuries) and family law (divorce, custody, child support).

Q: What are your business hours?
A: Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM. After-hours calls are handled by our answering service and returned next business day (emergencies same evening).

Q: How can I request support?
A: Call (214) 555-0312, email intake@carterassociateslaw.com, or submit a form at carterassociateslaw.com/free-consultation.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: PI cases are contingency (no upfront cost). Family law accepts credit card, ACH, check, or payment plan for retainers.

Q: How do I check my case status?
A: Log into the Clio Client Portal (link in your welcome email) or call the office.
