## Products and Services

### Homeowners Insurance
- Description: Coverage for dwelling, personal property, liability, and additional living expenses
- Carriers: Safeco, Travelers, Hartford, Progressive, Nationwide
- Average Premium: $1,800 - $4,500/year depending on home value and location
- Commission: 12-15% new, 10-12% renewal

### Auto Insurance
- Description: Liability, collision, comprehensive, uninsured motorist
- Carriers: Progressive, Safeco, Travelers, National General, Bristol West
- Average Premium: $1,200 - $3,000/year
- Commission: 10-14% new, 8-10% renewal

### Commercial Package
- Description: BOP (Business Owner's Policy), general liability, commercial property, workers comp
- Carriers: Hartford, Travelers, CNA, Employers, AmTrust
- Average Premium: $3,000 - $15,000/year
- Commission: 12-18% new, 10-15% renewal

### Umbrella / Excess Liability
- Description: Additional liability coverage above auto and home limits
- Carriers: Safeco, Travelers, RLI
- Average Premium: $200 - $600/year for $1M-$2M
- Commission: 15%

### Life Insurance
- Description: Term life, whole life, indexed universal life
- Carriers: Mutual of Omaha, Protective, North American
- Commission: 50-100% first year, 2-5% renewal

## Business Processes

### New Client Onboarding
1. Initial consultation (phone or in-person, 30 min)
2. Gather current declarations pages and driver info
3. Run quotes with minimum 3 carriers
4. Present comparison spreadsheet to client
5. Client selects carrier, sign applications
6. Bind coverage, collect payment
7. Send welcome packet with ID cards and policy summary
8. Schedule 90-day check-in

### Claims Process
1. Client calls to report claim
2. We document details and photos
3. File claim with carrier on client's behalf
4. Provide claim number and adjuster contact to client
5. Follow up within 48 hours
6. Advocate if claim is underpaid or denied
7. Document resolution in management system

### Renewal Process
1. Review 60 days before renewal
2. Check for rate increases over 10%
3. Re-shop if increase is significant
4. Contact client with renewal summary
5. Confirm no coverage changes needed
6. Process renewal or rewrite to new carrier

## Frequently Used Terms

- Dec Page: Declarations page — summary of policy coverage and limits
- BOP: Business Owner's Policy (combines property + liability)
- Binder: Temporary proof of insurance before policy is issued
- Endorsement: Change or addition to an existing policy
- Premium: Amount paid for insurance coverage
- Deductible: Amount client pays before insurance kicks in
- Loss Ratio: Claims paid vs. premium collected (carrier metric)
- Book of Business: Total portfolio of policies an agency manages
- Retention Rate: Percentage of clients who renew year over year
- Bind: To put coverage in force
