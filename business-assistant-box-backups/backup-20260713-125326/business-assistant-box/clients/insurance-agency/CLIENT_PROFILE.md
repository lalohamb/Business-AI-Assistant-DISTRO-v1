## Customer Service Philosophy

Insurance is confusing for most people. Our job is to make it simple. We never sell coverage someone doesn't need, and we always explain what's covered and what's not before they sign. When a claim happens, we're their advocate — not the carrier's.

## Common Questions

Q: How do I file a claim?
A: Call us first at (817) 555-0234. We'll guide you through the process and contact the carrier on your behalf.

Q: Can you save me money on my current policy?
A: Usually yes. We shop 20+ carriers to find the best rate for your coverage needs. Bring your current declarations page for a free comparison.

Q: What does my policy actually cover?
A: We'll do a full coverage review and explain everything in plain language. Schedule a 30-minute review anytime.

Q: Do I need an umbrella policy?
A: If you have assets to protect (home equity, savings, investments), an umbrella policy is one of the best values in insurance — typically $200-$400/year for $1M in coverage.
