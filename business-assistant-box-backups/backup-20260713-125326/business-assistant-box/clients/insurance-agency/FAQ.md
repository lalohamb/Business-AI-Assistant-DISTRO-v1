# FAQ.md

## Purpose

Quick answers to common questions for Pinnacle Insurance Group.

---

## Customers

Q: What services do we provide?
A: We shop 20+ insurance carriers to find the best home, auto, business, umbrella, and life insurance coverage at competitive rates.

Q: What are your business hours?
A: Monday-Friday 8:30 AM - 5:30 PM. After-hours claims can be reported directly to the carrier using the number on your ID card.

Q: How can I request support?
A: Call (817) 555-0234, email service@pinnacleinsurancegroup.com, or use the client portal on our website.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: Most carriers accept credit card, ACH, or check. Payment goes directly to the carrier, not to us.

Q: How do I get proof of insurance?
A: Call or email us and we'll send ID cards or a certificate of insurance within 1 business hour.

## Internal Staff

Q: Where are contracts stored?
A: HawkSoft for client policies, SharePoint > Contracts for vendor agreements.

Q: How are customer complaints handled?
A: Log in HawkSoft, notify Sandra within 2 hours, respond to client within 24 hours.

Q: How are refunds approved?
A: Policy cancellation refunds come from the carrier. Agency fee refunds require Sandra's approval.

Q: How do I process a policy change?
A: Pull up client in HawkSoft, process endorsement through carrier portal, update HawkSoft, email confirmation to client.
