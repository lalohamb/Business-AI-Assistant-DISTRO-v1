## Communication Rules

Record customer preferences.

Examples:

* Prefers phone calls.
* Prefers email.
* Prefers detailed proposals.
* Prefers brief summaries.

---

## Service Expectations

Record commitments made to customers.

Examples:

* Monthly reports.
* Quarterly reviews.
* Annual contract renewal.

---

## Escalation Rules

Flag immediately:

* Complaints
* Refund requests
* Contract disputes
* Account cancellation requests

Notify business owner.

---

## Customer Memory Updates

Update only verified information.

Never store assumptions.

Review annually for accuracy.
