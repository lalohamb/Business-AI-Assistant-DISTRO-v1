# CUSTOMER_RULES.md

## Purpose

Store rules about how specific customers or customer types should be handled.

---

## VIP Clients (Annual Premium > $5,000)

- Always route to Marcus directly
- Same-day response to all inquiries
- Annual review scheduled proactively (don't wait for them to call)
- Birthday card + handwritten note
- Invited to exclusive client appreciation dinner

## Senior Clients (Age 65+)

- Speak clearly and slowly on phone
- Always follow up phone conversations with written summary via mail (not just email)
- Be patient with technology — offer to handle paperwork for them
- Final expense focus unless they specifically request other products
- Never pressure for higher coverage amounts

## Business Owners

- Respect their time — be concise
- Lead with ROI and tax benefits
- Offer group benefits discussion at every touchpoint
- Schedule meetings early morning or late afternoon (they're busy during the day)

## Referred Clients

- Always mention who referred them in first contact
- Send thank-you to referrer within 24 hours
- Prioritize scheduling (treat as warm lead, not cold)
- Update referrer on outcome (with client permission)

## Previously Declined Clients

- Check if health has improved or time has passed
- Try different carriers (we have 30+)
- Consider guaranteed issue products as last resort
- Never make them feel rejected — frame as "finding the right fit"
