# OWNER_PREFERENCES.md

## Purpose

Store long-term owner preferences.

Only record information that improves future assistance.

---

## Communication Preferences

Preferred Email Tone: Professional and warm. Never cold or transactional.

Preferred Response Length: Concise but thorough. No fluff. Get to the point but don't skip important details.

Signature Style: "Protecting what matters most — Marcus D. Thompson, LifeLegacy Insurance"

---

## Email Rules

- Always include a warm greeting by first name
- Never send quotes without a disclaimer: "Rates shown are estimates pending underwriting approval"
- Claims-related emails must be empathetic first, procedural second
- All prospect follow-ups should include educational value (tip, article, or insight)
- Never use pushy sales language — educate, don't pressure
- BCC marcus@lifelegacyinsurance.com on all claim communications

---

## Scheduling Preferences

- No meetings before 9:00 AM
- Lunch block: 12:00–1:00 PM (never schedule)
- Preferred meeting length: 30 minutes for existing clients, 45 minutes for new prospects
- Friday afternoons reserved for team training and admin
- Community events only on Saturdays, max 2 per month

---

## Document Preferences

- Proposals use company letterhead template
- All documents saved to Google Drive under client folder
- Naming convention: YYYY-MM-DD_ClientLastName_DocumentType
- Annual review summaries must include: current coverage, gaps identified, recommendations

---

## Decision Rules

- Policies under $100k: Keisha can approve and submit
- Policies $100k+: Marcus reviews before submission
- Group benefits quotes: Marcus approves all
- Marketing spend over $500: Marcus approves
- Refund/complaint resolution: Marcus must be notified within 24 hours
- New carrier appointments: Marcus decision only

---

## Client Interaction Rules

- First-time callers always get a live person (no voicemail during business hours)
- Existing clients get callback within 4 hours
- Claims calls: drop everything, handle immediately
- Annual reviews: schedule proactively, don't wait for client to call
- Birthdays: send automated birthday email from CRM

---

## Content Preferences

- Social media: educational content only (no hard selling)
- Newsletter topics: financial literacy, policy tips, community events
- Tone for marketing: empowering, community-focused, family-oriented
- Never use fear-based messaging ("what if you die tomorrow")
- Preferred framing: "protecting your family's future" and "building generational wealth"

---

## Things to Never Do

- Never guarantee approval to a client
- Never discuss specific client information outside the office
- Never disparage competitors
- Never promise claim timelines (say "typically 30-60 days")
- Never email SSN, health records, or financial documents without encryption
- Never post client testimonials without written consent

---

## Owner Review Required

Owner reviews proposals before customer delivery.

Owner approves all marketing materials before distribution.

Owner signs off on all group benefits presentations.
