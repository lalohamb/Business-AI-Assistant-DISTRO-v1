# CUSTOMER_INTAKE.md

## Purpose

Define the customer intake and onboarding process for new LifeLegacy Insurance clients.

---

## Intake Sources

- Website contact form
- Phone call
- Referral from existing client
- Community event sign-up
- Social media inquiry

---

## Required Information (Needs Analysis)

### Personal
- Full name
- Date of birth
- Phone number
- Email address
- Mailing address
- Marital status
- Number of dependents (ages)

### Financial
- Annual household income
- Total outstanding debts (mortgage, auto, student loans, credit cards)
- Monthly expenses
- Existing life insurance coverage (carrier, amount, type)
- Retirement savings (approximate)
- Employer benefits (group life if any)

### Health (General — Not Medical Records)
- Tobacco use (last 12 months)
- General health status (excellent, good, fair)
- Major health conditions (diabetes, heart disease, cancer history)
- Current medications
- Height and weight

### Goals
- Primary reason for seeking coverage
- Desired coverage amount (if known)
- Budget for monthly premium
- Preferred coverage type (if known)
- Beneficiary preference

---

## Intake Process

1. **Initial Contact** — Respond within 4 business hours, schedule consultation
2. **Consultation** — Conduct needs analysis (phone/Zoom/in-person, 45 min)
3. **Recommendation** — Present 2-3 carrier options with coverage and premium comparison
4. **Application** — Complete e-application with client (assisted)
5. **Submission** — Submit to carrier, set follow-up reminder for 7 days
6. **Underwriting** — Monitor status, respond to carrier requests within 24 hours
7. **Approval** — Notify client, schedule policy delivery
8. **Delivery** — Review policy, confirm beneficiaries, explain payment schedule
9. **Onboarding** — Welcome email, add to CRM, schedule 30-day check-in
10. **Referral Ask** — At 30-day check-in, ask for referrals

---

## CRM Entry

Create new record in AgencyBloc with:
- Contact information
- Lead source
- Consultation date
- Needs analysis results
- Recommended coverage
- Application status
- Policy number (once issued)
- Annual review date (policy anniversary + 30 days)

---

## Follow-Up Schedule

| Timing | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Same day | Send confirmation email with consultation details |
| After consultation | Send recommendations within 48 hours |
| Application submitted | Notify client, set 7-day follow-up |
| Weekly during underwriting | Status update to client |
| Policy issued | Schedule delivery within 3 business days |
| 30 days post-delivery | Check-in call, referral request |
| 6 months | Mid-year check-in |
| 12 months | Annual review |

---

## Red Flags (Escalate to Marcus)

- Client mentions existing policy replacement (could be churning concern)
- Client requests coverage over $1,000,000 (enhanced underwriting likely)
- Client mentions legal proceedings or divorce
- Client under 18 or over 80 (special handling required)
- Client expresses financial hardship (may need smaller coverage, don't oversell)
