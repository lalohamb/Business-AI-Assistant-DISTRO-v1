## Customers

Q: What services do we provide?
A: Residential and commercial roof replacement, storm damage repair, gutter installation, and exterior siding.

Q: What are your business hours?
A: Monday-Friday 7:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Saturday 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM.

Q: How can I request support?
A: Call (214) 555-0187, text the same number, or submit a form at acmeroofingexteriors.com/contact.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: Check, credit card (3% processing fee), ACH transfer, and insurance direct pay.

Q: Do you finance?
A: Yes, we offer 12-month same-as-cash financing through GreenSky for qualified homeowners.

## Internal Staff

Q: Where are contracts stored?
A: JobNimbus (primary) and Google Drive > Contracts folder (backup).

Q: How are customer complaints handled?
A: Log in JobNimbus, notify Marcus within 1 hour, schedule callback within 24 hours.

Q: How are refunds approved?
A: Marcus approves all refunds. No refunds over $500 without written documentation of issue.

Q: How do I schedule a crew?
A: Use the Crew Calendar in JobNimbus. Check material availability first.
