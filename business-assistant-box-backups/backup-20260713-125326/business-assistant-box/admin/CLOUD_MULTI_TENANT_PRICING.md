# Business Assistant Box — Pricing & Plans

---

## Plans

### Starter — $149/month

Your AI assistant that knows your business basics.

**Includes:**
- AI chat interface with your business knowledge built in
- Knowledge vault (up to 50 documents indexed)
- 1 automated workflow (choose: daily briefing, email triage, or document drafting)
- Custom subdomain: `yourname.ourdomain.com`
- SSL encrypted
- Self-serve vault editing
- Monthly re-index of your knowledge base

**Best for:** Solo operators, freelancers, 1-2 person shops

---

### Pro — $299/month

A full AI office assistant that handles your daily operations.

**Includes:**
- Everything in Starter
- Unlimited vault documents
- All 6 business workflows:
  - Email triage & drafting
  - Calendar management
  - Customer intake
  - Daily briefing generation
  - Document drafting
  - Report generation
- Nightly auto-indexing (knowledge stays current)
- Custom procedures & memory (AI learns your preferences)
- Owner preference profile
- FAQ & customer rules automation

**Best for:** Small businesses, 3-10 employees, service companies

---

### Enterprise — $499–799/month

Dedicated resources and custom automation for growing operations.

**Includes:**
- Everything in Pro
- Dedicated database (fully isolated infrastructure)
- Custom n8n workflows built for your business
- Multiple user seats (up to 5 included)
- Priority support & onboarding call
- Custom domain support (yourdomain.com)
- Optional: dedicated server isolation
- Quarterly strategy review

**Best for:** Multi-location businesses, high-volume operations, regulated industries

## FAQ

**How fast is setup?**
Starter: same day. Pro: 1-2 business days. Enterprise: 3-5 business days with onboarding call.

**Can I upgrade later?**
Yes. Upgrade anytime — your data and vault carry over seamlessly.

**What if I cancel?**
You receive a full export of your vault, documents, and database. No lock-in.

**Do you have annual pricing?**
Yes. Pay annually and get 2 months free (effectively ~17% discount).

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| Starter | $149 | $124 |
| Pro | $299 | $249 |
| Enterprise | $499+ | Custom |

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Each client runs on isolated infrastructure. Your documents, conversations, and knowledge vault are never shared or used to train models.
