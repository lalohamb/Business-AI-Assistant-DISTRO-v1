# CALENDAR.md

## Purpose

Define how the assistant manages calendar and scheduling for LifeLegacy Insurance.

---

## Scheduling Rules

- Business hours: Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM EST
- No meetings before 9:00 AM
- Lunch block: 12:00–1:00 PM (never schedule)
- Friday afternoons (1:00–5:00 PM): reserved for team training and admin
- Maximum 6 client meetings per day
- 15-minute buffer between all meetings

---

## Meeting Types

### New Prospect Consultation
- Duration: 45 minutes
- Location: Phone, Zoom, or in-office
- Prep: Pull needs analysis worksheet, check if referral source noted
- Follow-up: Send recap email within 24 hours with recommendations

### Existing Client Annual Review
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Location: Phone or Zoom
- Prep: Pull current policy summary from AgencyBloc, check beneficiary info
- Follow-up: Update CRM, send summary email

### Policy Delivery
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Location: Phone, Zoom, or in-person
- Prep: Print/prepare policy documents, delivery receipt
- Follow-up: Send welcome packet, schedule 30-day check-in

### Group Benefits Presentation
- Duration: 60 minutes
- Location: At employer's office or Zoom
- Prep: Custom proposal deck, rate comparison, enrollment forms
- Follow-up: Send proposal PDF, schedule decision follow-up in 1 week
- Note: Marcus must attend all group presentations

### Team Meeting
- Duration: 60 minutes
- Frequency: Every Monday 9:00 AM
- Location: Office
- Agenda: Pipeline review, pending apps status, week priorities

---

## Conflict Resolution

- If double-booked: prioritize claims and existing clients over new prospects
- If Marcus unavailable: Keisha can cover client meetings (not group benefits)
- If running late: notify client 15 minutes before scheduled time

---

## Community Events

- Maximum 2 per month (Saturdays only)
- Require 2-week advance planning
- Book venue, prepare materials, confirm RSVP list
- Types: "Protect Your Family" workshops, church partnerships, community health fairs

---

## Daily Schedule Summary

Generate a daily briefing by 8:00 AM including:
1. Today's appointments with prep notes
2. Follow-ups due today
3. Pending application status updates
4. Any birthday or anniversary reminders
