## Common Questions

Q: How much life insurance do I need?
A: A general guideline is 10-12x your annual income, but we do a personalized needs analysis factoring in debts, dependents, income replacement, and future goals.

Q: What's the difference between term and whole life?
A: Term covers you for a set period (10-30 years) at a lower cost. Whole life covers you for your entire life and builds cash value you can borrow against.

Q: Can I get coverage if I have health issues?
A: Yes. We work with over 30 carriers and specialize in finding coverage for clients with diabetes, high blood pressure, and other conditions.

Q: How long does it take to get approved?
A: Most applications are processed within 2-4 weeks. Some carriers offer instant decision for healthy applicants.

Q: Do you charge a fee for consultations?
A: Never. Our consultations and needs analyses are always free.
