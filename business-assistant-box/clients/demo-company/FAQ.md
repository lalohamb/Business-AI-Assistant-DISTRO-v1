# FAQ.md

## Purpose

Provide quick answers to common questions.

Used internally by the assistant.

---

## Customers

Q: What services do we provide?

A: We provide term life insurance, whole life insurance, final expense/burial insurance, indexed universal life (IUL), group life benefits for businesses, annuities, and free policy reviews and needs analyses.

---

Q: What are your business hours?

A: Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time.

---

Q: Do you charge for consultations?

A: Never. All consultations and needs analyses are completely free with no obligation.

---

Q: How much life insurance do I need?

A: A common guideline is 10-12 times your annual income, but we conduct a personalized needs analysis considering your debts, number of dependents, income replacement needs, children's education costs, and future financial goals.

---

Q: Can I get coverage with pre-existing health conditions?

A: Yes. We work with over 30 carriers and specialize in finding competitive coverage for clients with diabetes, high blood pressure, heart conditions, cancer history, and other health issues.

---

Q: What's the difference between term and whole life?

A: Term life covers you for a specific period (10, 20, or 30 years) at a lower monthly cost. Whole life provides lifetime coverage and builds cash value you can borrow against or use for retirement supplementation.

---

Q: How long does approval take?

A: Most applications are processed within 2-4 weeks. Some carriers offer instant or accelerated decisions for healthy applicants, sometimes within 24-48 hours.

---

Q: What happens if I miss a premium payment?

A: Most policies have a 30-31 day grace period. We proactively contact clients 10 days before the grace period ends to help prevent lapses.

---

Q: How do I file a claim?

A: Contact our office immediately. We handle the entire claims process for beneficiaries — gathering forms, coordinating with the carrier, and following up until the benefit is paid. Claims are typically paid within 30-60 days.

---

Q: Do you offer coverage for my employees?

A: Yes. We offer group life benefits for businesses with 5 or more employees, including employer-paid and voluntary supplemental options.

---

Q: What areas do you serve?

A: We are licensed in Georgia, Florida, Alabama, South Carolina, and Tennessee. Most business is conducted over phone and video, so we serve clients across all five states.

---

Q: How does your referral program work?

A: For every referral that results in an issued policy, we send you a $50 gift card as a thank you.

---

## Internal

Q: How are customer complaints handled?

A: All complaints are logged in AgencyBloc, acknowledged within 4 business hours, and escalated to Marcus if unresolved within 24 hours. Resolution target is 72 hours.

---

Q: How are refunds approved?

A: Premium refunds are handled by the carrier, not our agency. We assist clients in requesting refunds for overpayment or cancelled policies by contacting the carrier on their behalf.

---

Q: What is our lapse prevention process?

A: We monitor policy status in AgencyBloc. When a policy shows pending lapse, we contact the client by phone and email 10 days before the grace period expires. If no response, we try again 5 days before expiration.

---

Q: What is our annual review process?

A: Every client receives an annual review call to confirm beneficiary information is correct, assess if coverage needs have changed (new baby, new mortgage, retirement), and check if rates can be improved.
