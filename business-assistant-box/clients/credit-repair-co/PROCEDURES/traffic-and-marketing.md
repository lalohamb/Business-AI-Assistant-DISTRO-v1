# PROCEDURES/traffic-and-marketing.md

## Purpose

Define the marketing strategy for generating traffic to the credit repair business and document how OpenClaw supports each channel.

---

## Marketing Goal

Drive qualified prospects — people with $10K+ in debt, in consolidation programs, or paying $250+/month toward debt — to the business through multiple traffic channels, converting them into leads with full contact information.

---

## Channel 1 — Google Business Profile (Free)

### What It Is
A free Google listing that appears when people search "debt consolidation [city]" or "credit repair near me."

### OpenClaw Support
- Draft responses to Google reviews (positive and negative)
- Write the business description and service listings
- Draft Q&A responses for the profile

### Owner Action Required
- Create and verify the Google Business Profile
- Post OpenClaw-drafted content weekly
- Respond to reviews using OpenClaw drafts

---

## Channel 2 — Organic Social Media (Free)

### Platforms
- Facebook — debt tips, success stories, myth-busting posts
- TikTok / Instagram Reels — short videos about debt relief
- YouTube — longer explainer videos that build trust and rank in search

### Content Themes
- "Did you know you can dispute collections legally?"
- "3 signs you need debt consolidation"
- "What happens if you ignore $10K in credit card debt"
- "How we helped a client go from $14K in debt to debt-free in 18 months"
- Myth vs. fact posts about credit repair

### Posting Frequency
- Facebook: 4–5 times per week
- Instagram: 3–4 times per week
- TikTok: 3–5 times per week
- YouTube: 1 video per week minimum

### OpenClaw Support
- Draft weekly social post calendar saved to `OUTPUTS/drafts/social/`
- Write captions, hooks, and calls to action for each post
- Write YouTube video scripts
- Write image prompt descriptions for Canva or AI image tools
- All drafts require human review before posting

### Output Location
`clients/credit-repair-co/OUTPUTS/drafts/social/[platform]-posts-[date].md`

---

## Channel 3 — SEO Blog Content (Free, Long-Term)

### What It Is
Articles written to rank in Google for searches people make when they have debt problems.

### Target Keywords
- "how to get out of $10,000 in credit card debt"
- "is debt consolidation worth it"
- "how to stop collection calls legally"
- "debt management plan vs debt consolidation loan"
- "credit repair after debt consolidation"
- "how long does debt consolidation take"

### OpenClaw Support
- Research and draft SEO articles using business knowledge as context
- Save drafts to `OUTPUTS/drafts/blog/`
- Write meta descriptions and page titles for each article
- Suggest internal linking between articles
- All drafts require human review before publishing

### Output Location
`clients/credit-repair-co/OUTPUTS/drafts/blog/[article-title]-[date].md`

---

## Channel 4 — Reddit and Quora Participation (Free)

### What It Is
Genuine helpful participation in communities where people ask about debt. Not spam — real answers that build credibility and include the business link where appropriate.

### Target Communities
- Reddit: r/personalfinance, r/debtfree, r/CRedit, r/povertyfinance
- Quora: Questions about debt consolidation, credit repair, getting out of debt

### OpenClaw Support
- Monitor these communities during lead search sessions
- Draft helpful responses to relevant questions
- Flag threads where a direct outreach may be appropriate
- Save response drafts to `OUTPUTS/drafts/community/`
- All responses require human review before posting

---

## Channel 5 — Paid Google Search Ads

### What It Is
Ads that appear when someone searches high-intent debt keywords on Google.

### Target Keywords for Ads
- "debt consolidation help"
- "get out of credit card debt"
- "debt relief program"
- "credit card debt $10000"
- "debt management plan"

### Estimated Cost
- $5–$25 per click depending on competition and location
- Start with $300–$500/month test budget before scaling

### OpenClaw Support
- Write ad headlines and descriptions (up to 15 headline variations, 4 description variations per Google's format)
- Write multiple ad group variations for A/B testing
- Draft landing page copy that matches the ad message
- Save all ad copy to `OUTPUTS/drafts/ads/google-ads-[date].md`
- All copy requires human review before launching in Google Ads Manager

---

## Channel 6 — Paid Facebook and Instagram Ads

### What It Is
Paid ads targeting people by financial behavior, age, income, and interests on Meta platforms.

### Audience Targeting Brief
- Age: 25–55
- Interests: debt relief, personal finance, budgeting, credit repair
- Behaviors: financially stressed consumers, people researching loans
- Location: [target city/region]
- Lookalike audiences: built from enrolled client list once available

### Estimated Cost
- $0.50–$3.00 per click
- Start with $200–$400/month test budget

### OpenClaw Support
- Write ad copy variations (see `PROCEDURES/social-ads.md` for full detail)
- Write audience targeting briefs
- Draft image prompt descriptions
- Save all to `OUTPUTS/drafts/ads/`
- All ads require human review before publishing in Meta Ads Manager

---

## Channel 7 — Referral Partner Network

### What It Is
Relationships with professionals who regularly encounter people with $10K+ in debt.

### Target Partners
- Mortgage brokers
- Auto dealers / finance managers
- Real estate agents
- Tax preparers / CPAs
- Bankruptcy attorneys

### OpenClaw Support
- Draft initial outreach emails to potential partners (see `PROCEDURES/referral-outreach.md`)
- Maintain partner contact list at `OUTPUTS/referral-partners.csv`
- Draft thank-you messages when a referral enrolls
- Draft monthly check-in messages to keep relationships warm
- All outreach requires human review before sending

---

## Priority Order (Start Here)

If starting from zero, execute channels in this order:

1. Google Business Profile — free, immediate local visibility
2. Referral partner outreach — free, highest converting leads
3. Organic social media — free, builds over time
4. Reddit / Quora participation — free, builds credibility
5. SEO blog content — free, long-term compounding traffic
6. Paid Facebook / Instagram ads — add once organic is running
7. Paid Google Search ads — add once landing page is tested and converting

---

## OpenClaw Weekly Marketing Tasks

Each week OpenClaw will, when instructed:

1. Draft social media post calendar for the week
2. Draft one SEO blog article
3. Check Reddit/Quora for community participation opportunities
4. Draft any pending partner follow-up messages
5. Save all drafts to `OUTPUTS/drafts/` for owner review

Nothing is posted or sent without human approval.

---

## Compliance Reminder

All marketing content must comply with CROA.
Never promise specific credit score improvements in any ad, post, or article.
Never use testimonials that imply guaranteed results.
All content requires human review before publishing.
