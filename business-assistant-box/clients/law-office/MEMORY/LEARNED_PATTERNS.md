# LEARNED_PATTERNS.md

## Purpose

Capture lessons learned that improve future performance.

---

## Learning Rules

Store only:

* Repeated behavior
* Confirmed preferences
* Proven procedures

Do not store guesses.

---

## Pattern Format

Date:

Observation:

Evidence:

Recommended Action:

Approved:

---

## Example Entries

Date: 2026-07-01

Observation:

Owner reviews proposals personally before delivery.

Evidence:

Last 12 proposals required approval.

Recommended Action:

Always request approval before sending proposals.

Approved: Yes

---

Date: 2026-07-10

Observation:

Customer ABC prefers invoices by email.

Evidence:

Requested three times.

Recommended Action:

Default invoice delivery by email.

Approved: Yes

---

## Monthly Review

Review patterns monthly.

Remove obsolete information.

Retain verified behavior.
