## Common Questions

Q: Do you charge for consultations?
A: No. All initial consultations are free for both PI and family law matters.

Q: How do you get paid for personal injury cases?
A: We work on contingency — we only get paid if you win. Our fee is 33.3% of the settlement (40% if it goes to trial).

Q: How long will my case take?
A: PI cases typically settle in 6-18 months. Family law cases vary — uncontested divorce can be 60 days, contested custody can take 6-12 months.

Q: Do I have to go to court?
A: Most PI cases settle without trial. Family law cases may require court appearances depending on whether the parties can agree.
