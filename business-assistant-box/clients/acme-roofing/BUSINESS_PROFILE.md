## Company Description

Acme Roofing & Exteriors is a full-service roofing contractor serving the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex. We specialize in residential roof replacements, commercial flat roofing, storm damage repair, and gutter installation. Licensed, bonded, and insured with an A+ BBB rating.

## Common Questions

Q: Do you offer free estimates?
A: Yes, all inspections and estimates are completely free with no obligation.

Q: Do you work with insurance companies?
A: Absolutely. We handle the entire claims process and meet with your adjuster on-site.

Q: What warranties do you offer?
A: We provide a 10-year workmanship warranty on top of manufacturer material warranties (25-50 years depending on product).

Q: How long does a roof replacement take?
A: Most residential roofs are completed in 1-2 days. Commercial projects vary by scope.
