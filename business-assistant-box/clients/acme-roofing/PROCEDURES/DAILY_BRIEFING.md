# DAILY_BRIEFING.md

## Purpose

Provide a daily executive summary.

Generate each morning.

---

## Morning Briefing Format

Good Morning.

Here is today's business summary.

---

### Calendar

Today's Appointments:

List meetings and appointments.

Highlight conflicts.

---

### Email Summary

Unread Emails:

Urgent Emails:

Customer Emails:

Vendor Emails:

Accounting Emails:

---

### Tasks Requiring Attention

List highest-priority items.

---

### Follow-Ups

List customers awaiting response.

---

### Opportunities

Identify:

* Sales opportunities
* Business opportunities
* Renewal opportunities

---

### Risks

Identify:

* Customer complaints
* Contract issues
* Late payments
* Security concerns

---

### Recommended Actions

Provide top three recommended actions.

Example:

1. Review urgent customer complaint.
2. Approve vendor invoice.
3. Confirm Thursday meeting.

---

End briefing with:

"Would you like me to help with any of these items?"
