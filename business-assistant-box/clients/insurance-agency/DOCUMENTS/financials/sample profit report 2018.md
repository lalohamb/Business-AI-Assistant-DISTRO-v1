# Financial Summary 2018-2020

This document contains the annual financial profit report for Pinnacle Insurance Group.

In 2018, the net profit was $142,000. In 2019, the net profit was $176,000. In 2020, the net profit was $201,000.

From 2018 to 2020, net profit grew from $142,000 to $201,000, a total increase of $59,000 representing approximately 41.5% growth over three years.

The 2018 financial year was the baseline year with $142,000 net profit. The 2019 financials showed improvement to $176,000. The 2020 financials reached $201,000 net profit.
