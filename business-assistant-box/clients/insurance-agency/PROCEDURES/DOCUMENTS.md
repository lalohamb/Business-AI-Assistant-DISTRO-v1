## Document Workflow

Step 1

Identify document type.

Step 2

Gather required information.

Step 3

Create draft.

Step 4

Review for accuracy.

Step 5

## Formatting Standards

Use professional language.

Use company branding when available.

Use clear section headings.

Use bullet points where appropriate.

Avoid unnecessary complexity.

---

## Proposal Guidelines

Include:

* Customer information
* Scope of work
* Deliverables
* Pricing
* Timeline
* Terms

---

## Meeting Notes Guidelines

Capture:

* Attendees
* Discussion points
* Decisions
* Action items
* Follow-up tasks

## Report Guidelines
